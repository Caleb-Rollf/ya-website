Our Story

Where We
Come From

Yhali Robotics was founded by people who have spent their careers in manufacturing: running facilities, sourcing materials, solving the problems that come up when you're actually trying to build something.

Modern robotic systems have reached a point where domestic production is genuinely cost-competitive for a wider range of businesses than most people realize. We started this company to help those businesses act on that.

If you're thinking about building or moving production to the US, we're happy to have that conversation and help you figure out what it would actually take.

Founder & Creative Director

How We
Work

Most conversations start with something like "can we automate this" or "what would it actually cost to build here." We take it from there.

01

Assessment

We start by understanding your product, your volumes, and what you need a facility to do. From there we can give you a realistic picture of what automation would involve and what it would cost.

→
02

Design

We scope a system around your specific process: footprint, throughput, integration points. Everything is specified before anything is built.
